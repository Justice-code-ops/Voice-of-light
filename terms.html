By submitting this registration form, I acknowledge and agree to the following choir guidelines and standards:

Acceptance of Rules

I agree to abide by the constitution, rules, regulations, and decisions of the choir leadership. I understand that these are intended to promote discipline, unity, and excellence in the ministry.

Two-week Probation Period

I understand that my registration does not automatically grant me full membership. Every new member is required to undergo a two (2) week probation period, during which attendance, punctuality, conduct, willingness to learn, and commitment will be observed.

Audition

At the end of the probation period, I will undergo a vocal audition. The audition is intended to determine my voice part (Soprano, Alto, Tenor, or Bass), vocal ability, and overall readiness for active choir service.

Attendance

I agree to attend rehearsals, church services, concerts, and other official choir activities regularly. If I am unable to attend, I will notify the appropriate choir leader in advance whenever possible.

Punctuality

I understand that punctuality is an essential part of choir discipline and will make every effort to arrive on time for all rehearsals and scheduled events.

Christian Conduct

I will conduct myself respectfully and demonstrate good character both within and outside the choir. I will avoid any behavior that could bring disrepute to the choir or the church.

Respect for Leadership

I agree to respect and cooperate with the Choir Master, executives, sectional leaders, clergy, and all appointed leaders, accepting corrections and guidance in a humble and respectful manner.

Teamwork

I will work harmoniously with other choir members, avoid unnecessary conflicts, and contribute positively to the unity and growth of the choir.

Uniform and Appearance

Where applicable, I agree to comply with the choir's dress code, uniforms, and grooming standards during services, rehearsals, concerts, and official engagements.

Financial Obligations

I understand that I may be required to contribute to approved choir dues, uniforms, music materials, or other officially communicated expenses, where applicable.

Commitment to Growth

I am willing to learn new music, attend training sessions, participate in voice development exercises, and continuously improve my musical and spiritual abilities.

Accuracy of Information

I certify that the information provided in this registration form is true and complete to the best of my knowledge.

Membership Decision

I understand that successful completion of the probation period and audition does not automatically guarantee permanent membership. Final admission rests with the choir leadership based on the choir's standards and requirements.

Media Consent

I understand that photographs, videos, and audio recordings taken during choir rehearsals, services, concerts, and official events may be used by the church or choir for ministry, publicity, documentation, and social media purposes.

Agreement

By checking the box below and submitting this form, I confirm that I have read, understood, and agree to comply with these Terms & Conditions as a member or prospective member of the choir.
